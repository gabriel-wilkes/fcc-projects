Dr. Jordan B. Peterson

"Tell the truth, clean your room, sort yourself out"

Jordan Bernt Peterson is a Canadian clinical psychologist, cultural critic, and professor of psychology at the University of Toronto.

I was introduced to him through his excellent YouTube lectures.

Most lectures are an hour or more though so if you want a shorter intro this YouTube channel has good short clips from his lectures.

He has quite an online following, his Reddit page is a good place to learn more about him.
